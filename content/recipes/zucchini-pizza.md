## Ingredients
- 2 medium zucchinis
- ¼ tsp sea salt
- ½ tsp basil
- ½ tsp oregano
- ¼ tsp garlic powder (or 2 cloves, finely minced)
- ½ tsp rosemary
- Dash of black pepper
- A few shakes of red pepper flakes
- ½ cup almond flour
- 2 Tbsp psyllium husk (in 4 Tbsp water)

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **235 °C**.
2. Grate the zucchinis and toss with the sea salt. Boil to remove excess water, or wrap in a cloth and squeeze.
3. Mix all the ingredients and let rest for 10 min.
4. Divide into 4 parts and shape into bases on a lined tray.
5. Bake for **15-20 min**, then add your toppings.
