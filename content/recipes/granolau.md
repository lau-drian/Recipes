## Ingredients
**To bake**
- 2 cups almonds (or a mix of nuts)
- 2 cups sunflower seeds
- 3 Tbsp hemp seeds
- 1 Tbsp cinnamon
- ¾ tsp salt
- ¼ cup melted coconut oil
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

**After baking**
- ¼ cup desiccated coconut
- 3 Tbsp chia seeds

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **150 °C**.
2. Mix the baking ingredients and spread on a baking sheet.
3. Bake for **15 minutes**, stir, then bake another **15 minutes** (**30 minutes** total).
4. When it comes out of the oven, add the desiccated coconut and chia.
