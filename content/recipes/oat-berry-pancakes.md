## Ingredients
**Wet**
- 1 banana, mashed
- ⅔ cup almond milk
- ¼ cup fresh blueberries

**Dry**
- ½ cup oat flour
- ¼ cup whole wheat flour
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp cardamom and nutmeg
- ⅛ tsp sea salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Coconut oil, for the griddle

## Steps
1. Mix the dry and wet ingredients in separate bowls.
2. Make a small well in the dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients into the centre.
3. Stir until gently combined.
4. Scoop ½ cup of batter per pancake onto a greased griddle.
5. Cook for **2-3 min**, flip and cook **2 min** on the other side.
