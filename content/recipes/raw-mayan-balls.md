## Ingredients
- 2 cups almonds (or 1 cup oat flour + 1 cup almond flour)
- 3 Tbsp cacao
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 12-13 dates
- 1 Tbsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp cardamom
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper (for extra spice)
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp date water

## Steps
1. Blend all the ingredients into a sticky dough.
2. Roll into balls.
3. Freeze for **15 min** before serving.
