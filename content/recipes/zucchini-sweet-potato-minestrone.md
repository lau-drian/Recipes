## Ingredients
- 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 2 carrots, chopped
- ½ tsp sea salt
- 1 (28-ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped (about 2 Tbsp)
- 15-20 thyme sprigs, tied in a bundle
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 2 (14-ounce) cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (14-ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 medium sweet potato, spiralized
- 2 medium zucchini, spiralized
- Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- Parmesan cheese and/or pesto (optional, for serving)

## Steps
1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, ½ tsp salt and pepper to taste. Stir and cook until the onion is soft, about 8 min. Add the tomatoes, garlic, rosemary and thyme. Stir, then add the broth, beans, chickpeas and the sweet potato and zucchini noodles. Simmer for 20-30 min, until the vegetables are soft.
2. Season to taste and serve with freshly grated parmesan or dollops of pesto, if desired.
