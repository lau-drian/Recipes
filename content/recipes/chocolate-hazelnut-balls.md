## Ingredients
- 1 cup hazelnut meal
- 1 cup medjool dates
- 1 Tbsp cacao powder
- ¼ cup cacao nibs or chocolate chips
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp vanilla
- Pinch of cayenne pepper

## Steps
1. Mash the dates until they form a ball.
2. Add the cacao nibs, cacao powder and hazelnut meal.
3. Add more dates if the mix is too dry — it should form a doughy texture.
4. Form into discs (2.5 cm) and eat.
