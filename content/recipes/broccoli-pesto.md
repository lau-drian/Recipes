## Ingredients
- 2 cups broccoli
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 2 Tbsp water
- ½ cup hazelnuts or cashews
- 2 cloves garlic (baked or fried in olive oil, unpeeled)
- Pinch salt
- Pinch pepper, to taste

## Steps
1. Add all the ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth, scraping down as needed.
3. Taste, adjust salt and pepper, and toss with pasta or spread on toast.
