## Ingredients
- ½ onion, finely chopped
- 1 bell pepper, chopped
- ½ tsp cumin seeds
- Handful of cilantro, plus extra for garnish
- Bay leaves
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 350 g (1 pack) chopped tomatoes
- 4 eggs
- Optional: 2 Tbsp harissa sauce

## Steps
1. Dry roast the cumin seeds for 2 min.
2. Sauté the onion for 2 min.
3. Add the bell pepper and cilantro over high heat for 1-2 min.
4. Add the tomatoes, bay leaves, salt and pepper and cook over low heat for 15 min.
5. Once the sauce has thickened, make a well for each egg and cover until cooked.
6. Garnish with chopped cilantro, avocado and lemon juice (optional).
