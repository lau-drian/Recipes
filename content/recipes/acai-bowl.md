## Ingredients
- ½ cup coconut, almond or oat milk
- 1 tsp almond butter
- 1 banana
- ½ cup strawberries, blueberries or raspberries
- 1 Tbsp acai powder
- 1 ½ cups water
- 1 tsp cardamom or cinnamon

## Steps
1. Add all the ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and thick.
3. Pour into a bowl and add your favourite toppings.
