## Ingredients
**Berry filling**
- 4 cups berries (mixed or just one type)
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup

**Crumble topping**
- 2 ½ cups oats
- 5 Tbsp almond flour
- Pinch sea salt
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 2 Tbsp maple syrup/date syrup
- 4 Tbsp coconut oil    

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees.
2. Mix berries with vanilla, lemon & syrup.
3. Prepare crumble separately (oats, flour, salt and vanilla).
4. Add maple syrup and coconut oil.
5. Pour berry mixture on a greased pan, then add crumble.
6. Bake for **35-40 min** until fruits are bubbling around the edges and the crumble is firm and lightly browned.
