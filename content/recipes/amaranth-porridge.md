## Ingredients
- ½ cup amaranth
- ⅓ cup coconut flakes
- 1 cup coconut milk
- ½ cup water
- Pinch of salt
- 1 date or maple syrup
- Berries, to top

## Steps
1. Rinse and drain the amaranth.
2. Boil the milk, water and salt.
3. Add the amaranth once boiling.
4. Reduce the heat, cover and cook for **25 min**.
5. Stir in the coconut flakes and sweeten with the date or maple syrup.
6. Top with berries.
