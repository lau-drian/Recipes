## Ingredients
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp ginger powder
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes
- 3 Tbsp tamari
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp water
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup (optional)

## Steps
1. Whisk all the ingredients together until smooth.
2. Add to a stir fry in the last minute of cooking, or use as a drizzle.
