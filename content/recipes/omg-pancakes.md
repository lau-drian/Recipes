## Ingredients
- 2 ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder (skip if your flour already has it)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp cinnamon, cardamom or licorice
- 6 Tbsp gluten-free flour
- Optional: chocolate chips, berries

## Steps
1. Mash the bananas.
2. Whisk in the eggs and the rest of the ingredients.
3. Add the flour one tablespoon at a time until the batter is balanced — not too runny, not too dry.
4. Add more flour if needed (we've gone up to 9 tablespoons).
5. Cook the pancakes in a hot pan until golden on both sides.
