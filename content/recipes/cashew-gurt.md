## Ingredients
- 1 ½ cups cashews, soaked 8-12 hours
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice (plus more to taste)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 10 Tbsp water
- 2 dates
- 1 pinch sea salt (depending on whether the cashews are salted)
- ½ tsp psyllium husk + ½ cup water

## Steps
1. Blend the cashews.
2. Add the lemon juice, vanilla, water and dates and blend until smooth (mix 1).
3. Separately, stir the psyllium husk with the ½ cup water until it thickens (mix 2).
4. Combine mix 1 and mix 2 in a bowl and stir until smooth.
5. Serve in bowls and chill in the fridge for about 15-20 min.
