## Ingredients
- 2 bananas
- 2 cups frozen strawberries or berries
- 2 Tbsp light coconut milk
- 2 Tbsp chia seeds
- ½ tsp cinnamon

## Steps
1. Combine the fruit and coconut milk in a blender.
2. Add the chia seeds and pulse to combine.
3. Cover and refrigerate for at least **2 hours**.
