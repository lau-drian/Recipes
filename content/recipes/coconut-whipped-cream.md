## Ingredients
- 1 can full-fat coconut cream, chilled overnight
- ½ tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp maple syrup

## Steps
1. Chill the mixing bowl for 10 min.
2. Add the coconut cream, vanilla and maple syrup and beat until creamy.
3. Can be stored for 1-2 weeks.
