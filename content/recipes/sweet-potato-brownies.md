## Ingredients
- 1 cup oats, ground
- 1 cup almonds, ground
- 6 Tbsp cacao
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, roasted
- 2 Tbsp coconut oil

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **180 °C**.
2. Mix all the dry ingredients, then add the roasted sweet potatoes and coconut oil.
3. Line a tin with parchment paper and spread the mixture evenly.
4. Bake for **45-50 min**, until a fork comes out dry.
