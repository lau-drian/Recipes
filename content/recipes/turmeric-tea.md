## Ingredients
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 piece of grated ginger (2 cm)
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 cups water

## Steps
1. Bring the water to a simmer.
2. Add the turmeric and ginger and simmer for **5 min**.
3. Remove from the heat and stir in the lemon juice and honey.
4. Strain and serve warm.
