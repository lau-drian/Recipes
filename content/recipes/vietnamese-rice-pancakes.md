## Ingredients
- 1 large Vietnamese banana
- 1 cup rice flour
- ⅓ cup condensed milk
- 1 egg
- 1 ½ cups water

## Steps
1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl. The batter shouldn't be too thin; if it's too thick, add a little water.
2. Heat coconut oil in a pan.
3. Once hot, pour the batter evenly into the pan.
4. Cook on both sides until golden.
