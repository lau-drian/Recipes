## Ingredients
- ¾ cup buckwheat flour
- ¼–½ cup almond meal
- ¼ cup cacao
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of sea salt
- 1-2 Tbsp date water
- 2 Tbsp date syrup
- 1 cup (about 10) dates

## Steps
1. Blend all the ingredients into a dough, adding the date water as needed.
2. Roll out the dough.
3. Freeze, then cut into cookies.
