## Ingredients
**Mix 1**
- 3 cups oats
- ⅓ cup uncooked quinoa
- 1 cup raw cashews
- ⅓ cup pumpkin seeds
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp pink Himalayan salt
- 1 heaped tsp cinnamon

**Mix 2**
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ cup coconut oil
- ⅓ cup maple syrup (or less)

**To finish**
- ½ cup unsweetened coconut flakes

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **175 °C**.
2. Combine Mix 1 and Mix 2 separately, then pour Mix 1 into Mix 2 and stir until well combined.
3. Spread on a baking sheet and bake for **15 min**, then remove, stir and shuffle around.
4. Turn off the oven, sprinkle the coconut flakes and bake for **5 more min** (**20 min** total), checking so it doesn't burn.
