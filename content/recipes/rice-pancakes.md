## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup red rice flour (or self-raising gluten-free flour)
- 1 cup plant milk
- 1 cup water
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp coconut oil
- Lemon zest
- ½ lime, juiced

## Steps
1. Blend all the ingredients until smooth.
2. Let the batter rest in the fridge.
3. Heat a pan with a little coconut oil and cook the pancakes until golden on both sides.
