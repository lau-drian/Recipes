## Ingredients
- 1 Tbsp oil (or sub water)
- 1 onion diced
- 1 bell pepper diced
- ½ tsp each sea salt + black pepper (more to taste)
- 500g dry black beans drained overnight 
	- Or sub for 2 cans black beans
- 6 cups of water with 3 veggie stock cubes
- 1 Tbsp nutritional yeast
- 2 Tbsp cacao powder
- 4 tsp ground cumin (for smokiness)
- 3 tsp chili powder (chipotle) or (½ tsp chili flakes)
- ½ tsp ground coriander
- ½ tsp paprika
- 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (optional)

## Steps
1. Sauté the onions.
2. Add the onion and all the ingredients to the crockpot. Leave it working.
3. **Manual** cook, high pressure, 35 min.
4. If we didn't soak the black beans, follow the same steps but with 45 min and an extra cup of water (7 instead of 6).
