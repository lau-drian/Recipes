## Ingredients
- 2 cups rhubarb, chopped
- ½ cup raspberries or strawberries
- 1 piece of ginger, grated
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup
- Orange zest (optional)
- 2 Tbsp chia seeds (optional)

## Steps
1. Place all the ingredients in a pot over medium-low heat.
2. Cook for about **20 min**, stirring occasionally.
3. Stir in the chia seeds at the end if you'd like a thicker jam.
4. Pour into a clean jar.
