## Ingredients
- 2 cups almond or oat milk
- 2 tsp turmeric (or 2 inches fresh turmeric, peeled and grated)
- 1 tsp ghee or coconut oil
- Pinch of black pepper
- 1 date (for VATA, instead of honey)
- Ginger and cardamom to taste

## Steps
1. Warm the milk in a small pot over medium heat (don't let it boil).
2. Whisk in the turmeric, ghee, black pepper, ginger and cardamom.
3. Simmer gently for **5 min**, stirring, until fragrant and warm.
4. Blend in the date (or stir well) until smooth, then serve.

> **Serving tip**
> The pinch of black pepper helps your body absorb the turmeric.
