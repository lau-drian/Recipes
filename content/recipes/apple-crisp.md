## Ingredients
- 3 apples, chopped and tossed with 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- ⅓ cup + ¼ cup oats
- ⅓ cup walnuts or almonds
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp cardamom
- ¼ tsp ginger
- 3 Tbsp coconut oil
- Pinch of salt

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **175 °C**.
2. Toss the chopped apples with the apple cider vinegar.
3. Mix the oats, nuts, spices, coconut oil and salt, then combine with the apples.
4. Brush a baking dish with coconut oil and add the mixture.
5. Bake for **35 min**, until the top is crisp and the interior is juicy.
