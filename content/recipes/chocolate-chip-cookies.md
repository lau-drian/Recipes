## Ingredients
- ½ cup vegan or dairy butter, at room temperature (or 3 Tbsp melted coconut oil)
- 1 heaping cup gluten-free baking mix
- ⅓ cup semisweet chocolate chips
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp sea salt
- ¼ cup aquafaba (or 1 large egg)

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **180 °C**.
2. Mix all the ingredients together, then refrigerate for **30 min**.
3. Scoop onto a lined baking sheet and bake for **12-15 min**.
4. Cool for 5 min before serving.
