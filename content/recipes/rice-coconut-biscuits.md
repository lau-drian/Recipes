## Ingredients
**Dry**
- 2 cups self-rising flour (or 2 cups rice flour + 1 tsp baking soda + 2 tsp baking powder)
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp sea salt

**Wet**
- ¼ cup solid coconut oil
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **218 °C**.
2. Mix the coconut milk and lemon juice to make buttermilk.
3. Mix all the dry ingredients.
4. Quickly work in the solid coconut oil so it doesn't warm up — it should resemble a sandy texture.
5. Make a well in the dry ingredients and pour in the buttermilk ¼ cup at a time.
6. Combine and stir gently; don't overdo it.
7. Shape into 2-inch circles about ½ inch thick and bake for **10 min**.
