## Ingredients
**Dry**
- 1½ cups oat flour
- ½ cup of almond meal
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp cardamom  
- ¼ tsp salt

**Wet**
- 1 Tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 mashed bananas
- 2 Tbsp almond milk (or more for extra smudginess)
- 1–2 dates for extra sweetness (optional)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees. 
2. Add dry ingredients to a large bowl and incorporate wet ingredients next 
3. Bake for **40-45 minutes**. 
4. Remove and let cool for 10 min before serving.


> **Baking tips**
> You can dismiss the ½ almond meal and use 2 full cups of oatmeal. 
> If almond milk is not available, you can sub with coconut or oat milk.
