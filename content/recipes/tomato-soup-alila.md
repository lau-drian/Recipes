## Ingredients
- 1 tsp butter
- ½ onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 tomatoes, chopped
- ½ carrot, chopped
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ tsp crushed black pepper
- Fresh basil leaves
- Basil oil
- 2 Tbsp cream

## Steps
1. In a pot, heat the butter and sauté the onion, garlic and bay leaf.
2. Add the tomatoes, carrot and salt. Sauté for a minute, or until the tomatoes change colour.
3. Add ½ cup water, cover and boil for 10 min, until the tomatoes are soft and mushy.
4. Remove the bay leaf and let the mixture cool.
5. Blend to a smooth paste without adding water, then sieve, discarding the residue.
6. Add ½ cup water (or more) to adjust the consistency.
7. Bring to a boil and add the sugar, pepper and ¼ tsp salt. Mix well.
8. Turn off the heat and stir in the cream.
9. Serve garnished with fresh basil and basil oil.
