## Ingredients
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp roasted sesame seeds
- 3 Tbsp tamari
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- 2 shakes ginger powder
- ½ lemon, juiced
- Pinch chilli flakes

## Steps
1. Whisk all the ingredients together until combined.
2. Taste and adjust, then drizzle over bowls, salads or stir fries.
