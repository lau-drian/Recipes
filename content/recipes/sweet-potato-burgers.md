## Ingredients
- 2 big sweet potatoes
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- ¼ cup cashews or almond butter
- ¼ cup chia seeds
- ½ tsp sea salt
- Freshly ground pepper
- 1 tsp melted coconut oil

## Steps
1. Brush the sweet potatoes with a little coconut oil and bake until tender.
2. Once cooled, peel and mash with a fork, adding the garlic, nuts, chia, salt and pepper.
3. Let rest for 20 min, then shape into patties.
