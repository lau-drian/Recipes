## Ingredients
- 1 ½ cups plant milk
- ⅓ cup chia seeds
- ¼ cup cacao
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ tsp vanilla
- Optional: 1 date

## Steps
1. Add all the ingredients to a bowl.
2. Whisk vigorously to combine.
3. Refrigerate overnight before serving.
