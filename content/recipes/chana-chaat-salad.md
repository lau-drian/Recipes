## Ingredients
- 3 cups cooked chickpeas
- 1 cup couscous, bulgur or quinoa
- 1 cup chopped cherry tomatoes
- Salad or spinach leaves
- Pomegranate seeds

**Dressing**
- 2 tsp lime juice
- ½ tsp cumin
- ¼ tsp red chilli powder
- Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Mix the chickpeas, couscous and cherry tomatoes, then add the salad or spinach leaves.
2. Sprinkle with pomegranate seeds.
3. Add the dressing and salt to taste.
