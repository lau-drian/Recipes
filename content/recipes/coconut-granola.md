## Ingredients
- 3 cups oats
- ¼ cup coconut oil (3 Tbsp)
- ½ cup shredded coconut flakes
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ cup raw pecan halves and slivered raw almonds
- ¼ tsp sea salt
- ¼ cup dried fruit (to add once out of the oven)
- Optional: maple or agave syrup, to taste

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to **150 °C**.
2. Melt the coconut oil and add the maple syrup, stirring for 2-3 min.
3. Mix all the dry ingredients (except the dried fruit) and add the oil and syrup.
4. Spread on a lined baking sheet and bake for **20 min**, then turn the pan, stir and bake for **7 more min** (**27 min** total), checking so it doesn't burn.
5. Add the dried fruit once it's out of the oven and no longer hot.
