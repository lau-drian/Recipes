## Ingredients
- 3 Tbsp chia seeds
- 1 cup water or plant milk
- 1 Tbsp creamy coconut milk
- 2 Tbsp orange juice
- Vanilla extract and cinnamon, to taste

## Steps
1. Mix all the ingredients well.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
