## Ingredients
- 1 cup green lentils rinsed
- 2 cups water with 3 cubes of veggie broth
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- ¼ onion finely chopped
- 1 minced garlic (1 tbsp garlic powder)
- 2 green bell pepper diced
- 1 can (425g) tomato sauce
- 2 tsp chilli powder 
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Pinch sea salt, pepper
- Handful cilantro & extra for garnish

## Steps

1. Sauté onion, garlic & bell pepper, stirring frequently until peppers are tender & onions slightly brown.
2. Add all ingredients to the crockpot.
3. Stir occasionally for 5-10 min.
4. Select **manual** high temperature for 16 min.
5. When the timer's done, wait for 20 min to release the pressure.
6. Taste and adjust seasonings, salt & pepper.
