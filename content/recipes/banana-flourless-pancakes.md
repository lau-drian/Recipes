## Ingredients
**Pancakes**
- 1 banana
- 2 eggs
- 2 Tbsp flaxseeds
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp cardamom
- ½ tsp salt

**Topping**
- 1 apple, chopped
- ½ lemon, juiced
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp cardamom
- Cacao nibs

## Steps
1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, then mix in the banana and the rest of the pancake ingredients.
3. Heat a pan with coconut oil over low-medium heat.
4. Pour ¼ cup of batter per pancake and cook for **2-3 min** each side.
5. For the topping, toss the chopped apple with the lemon juice, cinnamon and cardamom, spoon over the pancakes and finish with cacao nibs.
