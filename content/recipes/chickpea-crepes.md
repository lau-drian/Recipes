## Ingredients
- 250 g chickpea flour
- 600 ml sparkling mineral water
- ½ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp curry
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper
- ½ tsp ground ginger
- ½ tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- Pepper to taste
- Coconut oil for the pan

## Steps
1. Add all ingredients to a bowl.
2. Mix well and refrigerate the batter for **30 minutes**.
3. Heat a pan and add coconut oil.
4. Pour ⅓ cup of batter into the pan.
5. Cook until golden, then flip with a spatula.
