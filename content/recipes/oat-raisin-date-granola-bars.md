## Ingredients
- ½ cup raisins
- 1 cup oats
- ¼ cup walnuts
- ¼ cup cacao
- 1 cup (5-6) dates, mashed
- 1 Tbsp honey + ¼ cup peanut butter, melted over low heat
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp fine sea salt

## Steps
1. Mix all the ingredients together.
2. Press into a lined dish and refrigerate.
3. Cut into bars (or roll into balls) and eat.
