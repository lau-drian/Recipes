## Ingredients
- ½ cup finely grated cucumber
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- Juice of 1 lemon
- ½ Tbsp olive oil
- 1 grated garlic clove *(optional)*
- ¼ tsp sea salt
- 1 Tbsp chopped dill
- 1 Tbsp chopped mint *(optional)*

## Steps
1. Grate the cucumber and squeeze out the water (over the sink in its own skin, or with paper towels).
2. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, garlic (optional), olive oil, salt, and herbs.
3. Add chilli when serving.
