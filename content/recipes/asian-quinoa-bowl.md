## Ingredients
- Red cabbage
- Spinach
- 1 cup quinoa
- Asian dressing, to top

## Steps
1. Cook the quinoa and let it cool.
2. Shred the red cabbage and combine with the spinach and quinoa.
3. Top with Asian dressing and serve.
