## Ingredients
- 1 cup almond milk
- 5 Tbsp chia seeds
- 3 Tbsp oats or buckwheat
- Handful of berries
- Cacao nibs
- Cinnamon
- Cardamom
- Optional: 1 banana
- Optional: 1 Tbsp almond butter

## Steps
1. Combine all the ingredients in a jar and stir well.
2. Cover and let rest overnight in the fridge.
3. In the morning, top with grain-free granola or shredded coconut.
