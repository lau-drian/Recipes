## Ingredients
- 1 (12-ounce) package frozen mixed berries (or fruit of your choice)
- 1 Tbsp cornstarch
- ⅓ cup packed dark brown sugar
- ¼ cup oat flour
- ¼ cup rolled oats
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- ¼ tsp cinnamon
- 3 Tbsp vegan butter (or regular butter)

## Steps
1. Mix the frozen berries with the cornstarch and let sit for 20 min.
2. Meanwhile, in a separate bowl, mix the brown sugar, oat flour, oats, walnuts and cinnamon.
3. Chop the chilled butter into small pieces and work it into the oat mixture with a fork (or your fingers) until crumbly. Don't overmix.
4. Place the berries in individual skillets or ramekins and spoon the crumble on top.
5. Bake at **220 °C** for **20-30 min**, until the top is golden brown and the berries are bubbling.
6. Let cool for 15 min, top with ice cream and serve.
