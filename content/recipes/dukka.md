## Ingredients
- ½ kg wheat kernels, roasted until brown and easily crushed
- 175 g brown lentils, roasted
- 65 g dill seeds, roasted until aromatic
- 65 g cumin seeds, roasted (or 50 g ground cumin, roasted)
- 65 g coriander seeds, roasted until aromatic (or 50 g ground coriander, roasted)
- 200 g sumac
- 100 g unripe grape sumac (if available)
- 300 g roasted sesame seeds
- 1 Tbsp salt (adjust to taste)
- 1 tsp lemon salt
- Red chilli flakes or paprika, to taste

## Steps
1. Roast all the ingredients in a thick-bottomed pan over low heat, except the sumac, lemon salt and salt.
2. Once they cool down, add the remaining ingredients and finely grind the mix in a spice grinder.
3. Stir in extra sesame or pistachio to finish.

> **Serving tip:** Eat dipped in olive oil, Gazan-style, with bread.
