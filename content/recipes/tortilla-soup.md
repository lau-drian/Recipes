## Ingredients
- 1 kg Roma tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 2 onions, cut into wedges
- 3 garlic cloves, peeled
- 2 chillies of your choice
- 1 Tbsp chipotle
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1.5 L vegetable broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Avocado oil

## Steps
1. Cut the tomatoes and onions and char them.
2. Char the garlic and seeded chillies.
3. Let cool and blend everything together.
4. Heat a medium pot; once hot, add 2 Tbsp avocado oil.
5. Add the blended tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper.
6. Add chipotle and oregano.
7. Cook for **5 minutes**, then add the vegetable broth.
8. Let bubble for **10 minutes**.
9. Adjust seasoning (salt, pepper, oregano, or more chipotle).
10. Cut tortillas into strips.
11. Serve with lime, avocado, fresh cheese, cilantro, and lightly charred ancho chilli cut into strips.
