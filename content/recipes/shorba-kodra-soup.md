## Ingredients
- Carrot
- Pumpkin
- Broccoli
- Asparagus
- Spinach
- Onion
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Roasted cumin powder
- Roasted coriander powder
- Smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper
- Vegetable stock

## Steps
1. Heat the oil in a thick pan and gently fry the chopped onion and garlic for 5 min.
2. Add all the other vegetables except the spinach.
3. Add the cumin, coriander and smoked paprika.
4. Add the stock and cook for **35 min**, stirring several times.
5. When all the vegetables are cooked, take off the heat and add the spinach.
6. Blend until smooth and pass through a sieve.
7. Serve with chopped parsley and chili oil.
