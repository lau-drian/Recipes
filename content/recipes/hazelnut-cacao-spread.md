## Ingredients
- 2 cups hazelnuts
- ¼ cup date syrup
- 3 Tbsp cacao
- ½ cup water

## Steps
1. Bake the hazelnuts for **10 min** at **180 °C**, then peel them.
2. Process the hazelnuts for about 10 min until they turn into a smooth butter.
3. Mix in the date syrup and cacao until smooth.
4. Add the water gradually for a spreadable texture.
