## Ingredients
- 1 cup cashews or walnuts (or 4 heaped Tbsp peanut butter)
- 1 tsp each chilli, cumin and paprika
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 1 cup cooked rice or bulgur
- 1 can black beans
- ¼ onion, chopped

## Steps
1. Fry the onion and mix with the spices and crushed nuts (mix 1).
2. Mash the beans and add the rice or bulgur (mix 2).
3. Mix 1 and 2 together, adding a little bean water to make the texture smoother.
4. Shape into patties and cook in a hot pan until golden on both sides.
