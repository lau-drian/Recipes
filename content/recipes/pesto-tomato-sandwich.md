## Ingredients
- Green pesto
- Sun-dried tomatoes in oil
- Mozzarella cheese
- Sourdough bread
- Mushrooms *(optional)*

## Steps
1. Layer pesto, sun-dried tomatoes, mozzarella, and mushrooms if using on the bread.
2. Bake at **180 °C** for about **10 minutes**, until the cheese melts.
