## Ingredients
**Dry**
- ¼ cup coconut flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- Pinch of sea salt

**Wet**
- ⅓ cup almond milk
- ⅛ tsp almond extract
- ½ tsp vanilla extract
- 3 large eggs, at room temperature
- 3 Tbsp coconut oil
- 1 Tbsp honey

## Steps
1. Mix the coconut flour, baking powder and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, mix the coconut oil and honey, then whisk in one egg at a time.
3. Add the almond milk, extracts and lemon zest.
4. Mix well and cook the pancakes in a hot pan until golden on both sides.
