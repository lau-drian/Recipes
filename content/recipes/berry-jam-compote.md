## Ingredients
- 1 cup frozen berries
- ½ cup raspberries
- ¼ cup water
- 1 Tbsp chia seeds
- 1 Tbsp maple syrup (optional)

## Steps
1. Bring all the ingredients to a boil.
2. Simmer for **10 min**.
3. Pour into a clean jar.
