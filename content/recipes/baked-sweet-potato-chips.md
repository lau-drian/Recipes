## Ingredients
- 2 medium sweet potatoes
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F) and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Cut the sweet potatoes into thin slices, about 4 mm (⅛ inch).
3. Place the slices in a bowl, drizzle with oil and salt and toss until coated.
4. Spread them out on the baking sheets and bake for about **25 min** or until crispy, flipping halfway through.
5. Serve with hummus and shakshuka.
