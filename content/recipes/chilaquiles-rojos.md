## Ingredients
- 6 guajillo chiles
- 2 tomatoes
- Tortilla chips or fried tortillas
- Garlic
- Epazote
- Oil
- Salt or seasoning to taste

## Steps
1. Boil the chiles (tips removed) until soft, about 20 min over medium heat.
2. Devein the chiles: remove the tips, veins and seeds.
3. Blend the chiles with the tomatoes and garlic with half a litre of water, then strain.
4. Heat a little oil in a pot and season the sauce; add more water if it's too thick.
5. Once it boils, add a few sprigs of epazote and the seasoning (the epazote gives that chilaquiles flavour).
6. Add the tortilla chips when serving.
