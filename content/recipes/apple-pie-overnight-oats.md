## Ingredients
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 Tbsp chia seeds
- ½ cup milk
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ apple, chopped
- 1 Tbsp chopped nuts (to add in the morning)

## Steps
1. Add all the ingredients except the nuts to a jar in order and mix well.
2. Cover and let rest overnight in the fridge.
3. In the morning, top with the chopped nuts.
