## Ingredients
- 500 g small potatoes
- 2 Tbsp of salt
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Pinch sea salt, pepper


## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees
2. Cut potatoes in wedges
3. Bring salted water to boil 
4. Add potatoes and let simmer for **8 min**
5. Strain water and leave the potatoes  to dry for a few min
6. Put in a bowl with 2 tsp of olive oil 
7. Arrange in a baking sheet and grill for **30 min**
8. Add salt, pepper and paprika
9. Take them out when they look golden
