## Ingredients
- ½ cup cashew butter (if unsalted, add extra soy sauce)
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
- ¾ tsp chilli garlic sauce
- 2 Tbsp sweetener (maple syrup, agave or brown sugar)
- ½ medium lime, juiced
- Hot water, to thin

## Steps
1. Whisk together the cashew butter, soy sauce, chilli garlic sauce, sweetener and lime juice.
2. Add hot water a little at a time until you reach a pourable, creamy consistency.
3. Taste and adjust, then serve over noodles, bowls or veggies.
