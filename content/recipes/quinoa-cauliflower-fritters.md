## Ingredients
- 200 g quinoa (cooked with ½ L water and a pinch of salt)
- 200 g cauliflower rice
- 200 g feta cheese
- ¼ onion, finely chopped and sautéed
- 4 eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 80 g oats (¾ cup)
- Chopped cilantro *(optional)*

## Steps
1. Add the onions and eggs and stir.
2. Mix in the cooked quinoa, cauliflower, cheese, salt, and pepper.
3. Add the oats.
4. Rest in the fridge for **30 minutes**.
5. Cook in coconut oil; fry **3–4 minutes** per side.
