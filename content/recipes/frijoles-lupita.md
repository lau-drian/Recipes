## Ingredients
- Dried beans
- 1 onion
- 1 Tbsp oil
- Salt
- Epazote

## Steps
1. The night before, wash the beans and leave them to soak in hot water.
2. The next day, drain off that water and add fresh hot water.
3. Cook in a pot with the onion and a tablespoon of oil.
4. Keep adding hot water as the beans boil, since the liquid will reduce.
5. Once the beans are soft, add salt and epazote.
6. Let boil a little longer, then serve.
