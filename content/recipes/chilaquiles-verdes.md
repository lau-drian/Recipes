## Ingredients
- Tortillas
- 10 tomatillos
- 3 green chiles
- Cilantro sprigs
- ½ garlic clove
- Oil
- 1 cube of vegetable seasoning

## Steps
1. Boil the tomatillos and chiles in water.
2. Blend them with the cilantro and garlic with a little water so the sauce is thick.
3. Season and let simmer for 20 min over medium heat.
4. Warm the tortillas in the microwave or soften them in a pan with a little oil, then cut into triangles.
5. Dip the tortilla chips in the sauce and serve.
